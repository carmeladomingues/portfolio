About me

The story behind my
ux/ui design journey

Lisbon-based designer working across UX/UI, service design, inclusive products, and visual storytelling.

It all started on Tumblr in 2008... weird, I know, but I promise it's going to make sense.

I was just a teenager when I fell down the rabbit hole of edits and gifs around the internet. Something clicked. I taught myself Photoshop from scratch, late nights and endless YouTube tutorials. And just like that, a designer was born.

I graduated in Business at the University of Rio Grande do Sul. Not the obvious path, but it taught me to think about people, systems, and strategy, which turns out to be half of UX. My heart was always somewhere between pixels and people, so I chased it through internships and roles at companies like SAP and Telus, turning real problems into real solutions.

Born and raised in Brazil, that chase made me pack my life into suitcases and move to Lisbon to do a Master's in Design Management at IADE. Spoiler: it was the best idea I've ever had. But I believe this is just one stop on a longer journey.
