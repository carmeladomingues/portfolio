About me

The full story about my ux/ui design journey

It all started on Tumblr in 2008. Weird, I know, but I promise it's going to make sense.

I was just a teenager when I fell down the rabbit hole of edits and gifs floating around the internet. Something clicked. I taught myself Photoshop from scratch — late nights, endless tutorials, a lot of trial and error. And just like that, a designer was born.

I graduated in Business at the University of Rio Grande do Sul. Not the obvious path, but it taught me to think about people, systems, and strategy — which turns out to be half of UX.

Born and raised in Brazil, that chase eventually made me pack my life into suitcases and move to Lisbon to do a Master's in Design Management at IADE. Spoiler: best idea I ever had. But I believe this is just one stop on a longer journey.
